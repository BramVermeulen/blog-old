Webinar: AI in 2021

13/02/2021
Active Passive Webinar Job interview

How did I know about this webinar?

Another student informed be about this webinar on PlanetTalent and told me it would most likely be interesting because it was about AI and given by an ML6 employee, Florentijn Degroote.

Who are ML6?

ML6 is a team of machine learning experts who drive operational efficiency and value creation through self learning systems. They focus on staying on top of research, being innovative, cultivating talent and applying their research in practical situations.

Who are PlanetTalent?

PlanetTalent has an impact on the professional world of tomorrow. With their exclusive ecosystem around digital transformation, they offer companies a pool of young, digital talent & give ambitious start-ups a chance to navigate smartly through their careers and make an impact from day one.

What was the webinar about?

The first part of the webinar was an introduction to ML6 and what their business is all about. After that Florentijn went over the different types of AI.

The second part was focused on real use cases. The most intriguing example to me was the one about the soccer game because this is such a practical example and is used for an industry that most are at least familiar with.

Conclusion

This webinar learned me more about the practical use cases for AI and how it can improve our lives in unique ways. I would recommend this webinar to anyone who has some interest in how AI works.
