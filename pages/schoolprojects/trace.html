Trace

What is Trace?

The focus of this project was making a fictional product that we had to make a paper for and also make a pitch at the end of the semester. During the course of the semester we held meetings to work out our fictional concept. We chose to make Augemented Reality glasses that would work with a specially designed board game. This would allow the player interesting ways of interacting with the game.

The team

This project was a group project which I worked together on with four colleagues: Bert Van Mulders, Mattijs Step, Brend Lambert and Michael Spillier.
