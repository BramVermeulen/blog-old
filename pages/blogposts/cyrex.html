Webinar: Online Game Hacking

28/04/2021
Passive Hacking Gaming Webinar

Who are Cyrex?

Cyrex specializes in secure software development and application penetration testing. They pride themselves on their cutting-edge development and deep, technical security assessments.

What was the webinar about?

The first part of the webinar was an introduction to Cyrex and what their business is all about. After that Mathieu went over the different topics he will talk about. The table of contents can be seen in the image above.

The second part was focused on the six topics and the Q&A. Mathieu went over how anti-cheat is security by obscurity and that it just takes longer to find a way around the anti-cheat. He gave the example of Fortnite were the developers are playing a cat and mouse game with the hackers. They patch the game everytime a new bypass of the anti-cheat is found. Anti-cheat should not be your main way of securing your game.

Conclusion

This webinar learned me more about how games are hacked and how to prevent them from being hacked. I would recommend this webinar to anyone who has some interest in gamehacking from the perspective of the hacker and the gamedevelopers.
