New Horizon - Clash of Elements

What is New Horizon?

For this project we had to make to make a breakout variant and make a company which the mainfocus is not game development. This breakout needed to be multiplayer and you needed to be able to uses special powers/abilities. We chose to make a breakout variant which would focus on sensitizing people on the eniroment and finite resources. We also needed to make a business case and make a presentation for possible investors. The focus of this project was more on the business case than the game it self.

The team

This project was a group project which I worked together on with four colleagues: Sarah Polfliet, Gijs Dujardin, Vincent Smets and Rein Deroo. Our personal coach was Thomas Clauwaert.
