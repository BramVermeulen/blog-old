WIRE.HACK

20/04/2019
Active HTML CSS JS NodeJS PWA

What is WIRE.HACK?

WIRE.HACK was a 24-hour-long hackathon were we had to solve a certain IT related problem. We were assissted during this hackathon by technical specialists. At the start of the event we got a few tips when making our concept. After the hackathon we had to pitch our project to a jury. We were free to choose the programming languages.

The team

This project was a group project which I worked together on with two colleagues: Ruben Vermeulen and Brend Lambert.

The app

The theme for the hackathon was "Fintech". We made an app which would make doctor's prescription a lot easier for the doctor and the patient.

The businessmodel

The experience

This was my first ever hackathon, so it was a very interesting and new experience. The organizers of the hackathon made sure we had everything we needed to enable us to work the whole 24 hours. This event to this day remains one of the unique experiences I have had during my studies as a Software Engineer. Everybody as a Software Engineer, when given the chance, should at least once experience a 24-hour hackathon.
