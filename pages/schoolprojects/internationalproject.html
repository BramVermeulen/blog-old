International Project - Bryo Bootcamp

Which International Project?

For the international project I chose to go on Bryo StandUp Summer Bootcamp in Warsaw. But the international bootcamp was canceled due to Covid-19. Instead the organizers made an alternative version that would be hosted online.

In spite of the bootcamp not being international we still learned much about how to work out an original idea and how to actually let your product or service come to market and everything in between. This project was an unforgettable experience.
