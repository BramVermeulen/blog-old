Mars-IT: discord.py

What is Mars-IT?

Mars-IT was a conference were al the Software Engineering studenst had to present a trending topic we had used during our project. I demonstrated how to make a discord bot with the discord.py library and how to register your own bot.

Our project

Our project was making a fictional Market for the people on Mars to use. We each made a different piece of the project with our chosen trending topics. On this market you can buy and sell ores and materials, my discord bot was used as platform for the traders to discuss and get more information.

The team

This project was a group project which I worked together on with two colleagues: Lauren Schouppe and Brian Verbanck.
