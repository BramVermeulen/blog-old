LoREco frontend project

What is LoREco?

During this project we had to work for external clients and make a piece of software for them. Our client was Howest's Networkeconomy division LoREco researchproject. LoREco works on a way to integrate complementary currencies and an inteligent way of implementing a basic income.

Our project

The client asked of us to make a visually pleasing and intuitive frontend application for inovative currency exchange system. We had free choice of what technologies we could use. We chose to use React Native becuase the did niet need to be a website and we could make use of many of it's native functionlities. In spite of the bootcamp not being international we still learned much about how to work out an original idea and how to actually let your product or service come to market and everything in between. This project was an unforgettable experience.

The team

This project was a group project which I worked together on with one colleague: Arno Debeir. Our personal coach was Thijs Martens.
